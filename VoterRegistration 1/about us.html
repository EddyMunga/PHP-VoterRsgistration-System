Building the Political Power for all people

We are dedicated to building the political power managed by all the people.

We created this website in hopes to increase minority voter turnout numbers for the upcoming 2022 General Election. We also want to make it as easy as possible and show voters that this year they have options on how and when to register to vote.

Our system ensures That:

1.There are multiple methods of identifying voters uniquely
2.That capture of voters' records is fast, efficient and direct.
3.Security and privacy of information is enhanced.
4.Integrity and reliability of information is improved e.g. elimination of duplicates


Mission

To conduct transparent, efficient, and impartial Registration of voters; and undertake boundary delimitation for equitable representation and sustainable democracy


Vision

A credible electoral management body that meets the democratic aspirations of the people of Kenya


Core Values

Adherence to the rule of law - We conduct our businesses within the law.
Inclusivity - We respect gender, race, class and disability in the conduct of electoral activities.
Integrity - We conduct our affairs with utmost honesty.
Accountability - We endeavor to be responsible for our actions.
Teamwork - We work collaboratively as colleagues to achieve Commission goals.
Innovativeness - We transform the electoral process by exceeding the expectations of Kenyans.
